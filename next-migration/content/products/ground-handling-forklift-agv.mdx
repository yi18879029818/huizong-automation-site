## Operational narrative

Ground Handling Forklift AGV is designed for sites that need stable autonomous pallet movement at floor level without introducing a full high-rack forklift architecture. It closes the gap between manual pallet trucks and larger automated forklift fleets.

## Why it matters

- It introduces autonomous pallet transfer without overbuilding the system footprint.
- It protects shared aisles with layered sensing and adaptive obstacle avoidance.
- It creates a clean entry point for future fleet expansion and WMS-driven dispatch.
