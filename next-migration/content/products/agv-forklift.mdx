## Operational narrative

The AGV Forklift sits at the heavy-load edge of the product family. It is designed for facilities where pallet travel, rack putaway, and inbound-to-buffer movement all need to happen with minimal manual intervention.

## Why it matters

- It reduces dependency on manual forklift traffic in dense storage zones.
- It makes high-bay storage more predictable for both safety and release timing.
- It creates a clean control surface for later analytics and fleet-level orchestration.
